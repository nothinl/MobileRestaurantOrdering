About Bramps

Bramps is a restaurant that aims to provide delicious foods and high quality services in the Philippines. A group of students founded this restaurant as way to earn and provide the best quality foods that competes with other restaurants. We provide low priced delicious foods with specialty in burning chickens and sizzling sisigs.

Our main branch is located in Laguna, Philippines at the moment.
